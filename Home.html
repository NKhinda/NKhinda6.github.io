What is Virtual Reality? What is Augmented Reality?

Virutal reality is a gaming technology used to play videogames. Instead of playing in front of a screen, you are inside a 3d world. The screen of virtual reality is one the biggest differences from playing normal videogames.

Augmented reality is a big trend of technology. In Augmented reality, you get to see a realistic environment of the outside world.

What is the Equipment used in Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality?

The main equipment used in Virtual Reality is a headset and a pair of goggles. The goggles go over your eyes and act like a mask over your eyes. The goggles will show you all of the content in Vr. In order to run the games and apps in the system, your headset needs to be connected to a computer.

The main equipment required in Augmented Reality is a smartphone, ipad, or tablet. However, there are certain things you need on your device in order for Ar to work. On your device you need a processor, a high graphing process unit, and sensors. For the software part, you need environment understanding, motion tracking, and light estimation.

The History of Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality

The technologies of Virtual Reality were first developed in the 1800's. After discovering the equipment for virtual reality, Jaron Lanier decided to call it "virtual reality". Before virtual reality began, "technologists" had been developing different types of environments in a simulation.

In 1968, Ivan Sutherland invented a computer based environment. The display was "window-like". The first Augmented Reality device was invented in 1922. The fist system did have a few issues. The speed of the 3D graphics" were not the best. Throughout the years Ar has became a very popular system and the technology is getting more and more advanced.
